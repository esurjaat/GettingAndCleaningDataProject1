Independent Variables

ID: The Subject's ID Number.  Identifies the performers of the given exercises
        and who's results are measured by the measurement variables
Exercise:  The given task to the subject to perform


Dependent Variables:

        Components:
        -Variables that begin with t denote time measurements

        -Variables that begin with f denote frequency measurements

        -"Body" denotes the measurements attributed to the body

        -"Gravity" denotes the measurements attributed to gravity

        - "Acc" denotes acceleration measurements
        
        - "Mag" denotes magnitude measurements

        -X,Y & Z: Refers to the axis of direction

        -Mean: The mean measurement

        -Std: The standard deviation
